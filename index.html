What is the enigma?

The Enigma machine is an encryption device developed and used in the 30s and 40s of the 20th century to protect commercial, diplomatic and military communication. It was employed extensively by Nazi Germany during the secound world war, in all branches of the German military.

How do use this enigma?

Rotor:

With the three selects you can choose wich three of the five rotors you want to use. Please note you cannot use the same ones twice!

Position:

With the +/- Buttons you can set the inital possition of the three rotors. The position is shown between the buttons

UKW:

With the ukw select you can decide wich ukw you want to use for your encryption

Input-Switch

If the switch is brown you have the keyboard input activated, you can use the enigma by typing on your keyboard and the encrypted letter lights on

If the switch is green you have the text input activated, you can use the enigma by typing in a text in the left text area and encrypt it with the encrypt button, the encrypted text will be shown on the right textfield

Plugboard:

You can setup the plugboard by typing the two letters wich should be connected into one of the plugboard fields
